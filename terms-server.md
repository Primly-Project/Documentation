## §1 Acceptance

By joining and using this Discord server ("Server"), you agree to follow these Terms ("Server rules") and Discord's Terms of Service.

---

## §2 Community Conduct

Members must:

* Treat others with respect
* Comply with Discord's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
* Avoid disruptive, abusive, or harmful behavior

Server staff may take action against content or behavior that negatively affects the community.

---

## §3 User Responsibility

You are responsible for:

* Your messages, media, and other content
* Your interactions with other members
* Keeping your account secure

* User should NOT share any personal informations, server links, dangerous/illegal websites or apps nor content that may hurt server members

The Server Staff cannot control or verify everything posted by users.

---

## §4 Moderation

Server Staff may:

* Remove messages, media, or content
* Issue warnings
* Punish members
* Restrict access to channels or features

---

## §5 Changes to These Terms

These Terms may and will be updated at any time.
